Visual Checks

Good general condition with no damage, excessive dirt or rust.

Forks correctly positioned, not damaged, cracked, bent or worn.

Forks anchor pins secure, not worn, loose or bent.

Carridge plate-no damage or distortion, sitting square to mast and lubricated, end stop bolts engaged.

Mast-Functional test and no damage, distortion or cracks, slides intact and secure, end stops secure.

Back Rest extension/load guard-in good condition, secure with no distortion or cracks.

Lift chains-not damaged, worn or stretched, no broken links or rust, all pins in place.

Tyres-no damage, wear, cracks, splits or separation.

Wheels-undamaged, rust and all nuts secure in place.

Overhead Guard-secure, undamaged.

Energy Source-battery plug and connection correct, power cable intact, no exposed wires, battery secure and charging adequately.

Hydraulics-no damage or fluid leaks, no splits in hoses.

Identification/reg plate-intact, clean and legible.

Operators compartment-clean and no loose items.

Access-steps and grab handles in good condition.

Lights, windscreen & mirrors clean and undamaged.

Fire Extinguisher (if fitted) secure, charged and in date.

Operational Checks

Seat-Good condition, secure and adjustable.

Seatbelt, accessible, good condition & working.

Reversing alarm & horn working correctly.

Warning lights & lights working correctly.

Hydraulic controls- working smoothly and correctly.

Brakes-functional test and working correctly.

Clutch & Gears-functional test and working smoothly and correct.

Steering-functional test and working correctly with no excessive play.

Exhaust - no excessive smoke, sparks or flames.

Fire Extinguisher (if fitted) secure, charged and in date
