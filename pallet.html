Pallet Truck Pre-Use Checklist

Visual Checks

Handles

Fixed Securely. No flex or damage

Hydraulic Cylinder.

Clean and free from leaks.

Wheels

Free from damage. No nicks or cuts. Secured Firmly.

Forks

Free from damage. Parallel to eachother. Parallel to ground.

Tagged

Not in prohibition or out of service.

Operational Checks

Operation

Pump to full height and hold for 10 seconds. Forks should not drop. Check no oil from cylinder.

Operation

Release Pressure.
Forks should drop smoothly. No oil from cylinder. Forms should both be the same height.
